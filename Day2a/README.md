# Day 2a

## Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

### Objective

The objective of this activity was to understand the concept of 
Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) by comparing two different printers 
and calculating the total expenses over a period of 5 years.

### Printers Chosen

1. HP DeskJet 2820e (Inkjet)
2. Brother HL-L2460DW (Laser)

### Assumptions

- Comparison period: 5 years
- Pages printed per week: 750
- Weekly runtime: 40 hours
- Electricity cost: $0.30 per kWh
- Paper cost: $6 per ream

### Costs Considered

Fixed Costs:
- Printer purchase price

Variable Costs:
- Ink/Toner
- Paper
- Electricity
- Maintenance

### Findings

After comparing both printers, I found that the laser printer had a higher purchase price but lower running costs over time. The inkjet printer was cheaper to buy initially but required more frequent cartridge replacements.

### What I Learned

This activity taught me that buying the cheapest product does not always mean spending the least money in the long term. TCO helps us consider all costs before making a decision.
